ABOUT US


Sphere Construction Company was founded in 2008 by Ray Morris.Our first jobs included building gas stations, and we quickly added restaurants to our workload.

We opened our Greensboro office in 2008, our Tampa office in 2010, our Memphis, Nashville and Richmond offices in 2011 and our Kansas City branch in 2014.We maintain a General Contractors license in 47 states, and construction projects have now been completed in 46 states.

Our work is coordinated through our seven branch offices by 35 project managers, 99 job superintendents and 11 area supervisors.Sphere is proud of her long list of satisfied clients and repeat contracts, now totaling more than 5,000 construction projects. We have proven, throughout our 8 year history, that we deliver a great project, on time, and on budget.
